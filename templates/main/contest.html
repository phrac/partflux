Win a $50 Amazon Gift Card!

Every month we give away a $50 Amazon gift card just for contributing to Part Engine! We put eligible users in a hat and draw the name on the last day of every month and then announce the contest winner here.

So how do you enter to win?

It's pretty simple actually. Here are the steps:

Create an Account

If you don't already have one, you need to create an account. Don't worry, its free and easy, and we aren't going to spam you with junk you don't need

Earn Some Reputation Points

Start earning reputation points. You can get points for creating new parts, uploading images, adding part attributes, and lots of other actions. These points are used to determine your community trust levels.

Here's a quick breakdown of point values:
Create a new part
10 points
Add a part cross ref
5 points
Upload an image
3 points
Add a part attribute
2 points
Add a purchasing link
1 point

As you can see, you can easily get a large number of reputation points just by creating a part, adding an image, and adding some attributes to the new part.

Cross Your Fingers

On the last day of each month we will randomly select a user that has gained at least 50 reputation points within the month. You will be contacted via email so that we can work out where to send the prize.

Good luck, and thanks for being a part of the community!
